An imaginary time-line for my made-up guy
  • Year 0 Born in squalid conditions, young... let's call him K. Right, young K was a healthy, young boy, which was surprising, considering that his mother was very clearly malnourished and his father was very clearly dead.
  • Year 3 Up until now, he had successfully managed to avoid the fate of his mother and the rest of the denizens of the small hamlet he lived in: death.
  • Year 5 With his inexplicable health, K soon caught the eye of the roving bands of bandits that made their living off the highway that wound it's way a few miles away from the hamlet. Thus began a new chapter in K's life. He was now a highway robber, albeit an undersized one.
  • Year 10 Due to the general lack of knowledge about concepts such as calendars and dates prevalent in those enlightened times, we can only narrow the occurrence of a pivotal event in K's life down to his tenth year with any reasonable accuracy. He died.
